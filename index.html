INVEST IN CITIES OF FUTURE

UPSIDA is the nodal agency for developing industrial infrastructure in Uttar Pradesh and has been instrumental in keeping the State at the numero uno position among the states for several decades now.

Till date, UPSIDA has developed a record 154 industrial areas on approximately 43,788.54 acres of land across Uttar Pradesh.
